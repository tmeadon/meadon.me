This is a sample post so you can see how the series will look once you start publishing the real articles.

The idea behind the series is simple: I want to be more honest about how I am using AI to help me keep on top of reading that matters to me as a parent, while still keeping my own judgement, voice, and responsibility front and centre.

## Why I started doing this

Like a lot of parents, I do not have the same amount of spare time I used to. Reading still matters to me, but the pile keeps growing.

AI gives me a way to move faster without pretending I have more hours in the day than I actually do.

## What I want readers to get from this series

- A transparent look at my process
- A practical summary of the material I am reading
- A clear view of where AI helps and where it does not
- A record I can come back to later

## What a typical post might include

Each article in the series can follow the same rough pattern:

1. The problem or topic I am tackling
2. The prompts or process I used
3. The useful output
4. My own reflections and takeaways

## Closing thought

This first sample post is intentionally lightweight, but it should give you a good sense of the visual hierarchy, the series label, and the disclaimer treatment on the finished page.
